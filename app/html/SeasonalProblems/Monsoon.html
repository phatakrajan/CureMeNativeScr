Its Raining... Get your umbrellas and raincoats out.Beware this is very dagerous season. If you can can stay healthy then only you can enjoy the rains.

From the health point of view beginning and end of the rainy season are very troublesome.The initial days of the monsoon can cause problems for the body because of the sudden transition of from bone-dry heat to days soaked in a downpour. The pre-monsoon days packed with clouds causing intense suffocation, resulting in excessive sweating.

The body is weakened during the summer, as it is the period of dehydration. The digestive power is also weekened. Hence it is advisable to be moderate regarding diet during the rainy season.

  • One should abstain from sleeping during the daytime.
  • Avoid drinking untreated water as waterborne diseases are at its peak during this season.
  • Abstain from excercise and moving about in the sun after a spell of rain.
  • Use of honey liberally in your diet would be very helpful.
  • If the days are cooler due to heavy rains, one should take diet and drinks that are conspicuously sour, salty, and unctuous.
  • One can have fresh and hot vegetable soups.
  • One should wear light and clean apparel.
  • Try to keep your rooms devoid of humidity.
  • Do not allow water to stagnate in your compound. Such pools are breeding ground for mosquitoes and other insects.
