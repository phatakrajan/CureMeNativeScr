Summer Time !!!
Following are certain measures that you can take to combat the summer heat and stay healthy this season

  • Drink plenty of liquids like coconut water, sugarcane juice, fruit juice, buttermilk etc.
  • At least 10 glasses of water a day is must in this season.
  • Eat light food.
  • Avoid hot, spicy, oily meals and opt for foods high in water content including fruits, salads, soups and buttermilk.
  • Lassi made from churned curd, cream and ginger acts as re-hydrating agent in summer.
  • A raw mango with salt can quench excessive thirst.
  • Avoid alcoholic beverages and drinks containing caffeine(tea and coffee) which can make the condition for heat worse.
  • While going out use umbrella to block sun rays.
  • Do outdoor work in the early mornings or late evenings.
  • Take frequent baths and showers to cool your body and to keep it clean.
  • Wear light-colored, loose fitting cotton clothes.
  • Smoking can constrict blood vessels and impair the ability to acclimatize to heat, so avoid it.
  • Fans blow hot air and increase the amount of evaporation of sweat. So, air conditioners are better.
