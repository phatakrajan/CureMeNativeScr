Our body is programmed to take care of the changes, but occasionally you may have to aid the process of adjustment through food and suitable alterations in lifestyle. When a cold, dry winds blow, the body sense that something akin to itself has begun to dominate the scene.

  • Include sour, sweet, salted soup or meat of aquatic and marshy animals in the diet.
  • Try to include wine, vinegar, and honey in the diet.
  • Regularly massage the body with oils. Also apply oil on head
  • Use room heaters or spend time in heated chambers.
  • Try to expose the body to sun's rays.
  • Avoid light and cold foods. Also avoid bitter,pungent and stringent tastes.

  • Massaging the skin with mustard oil is good for skin during winter. Sunrays will enhance the the good effects if this procedure.
