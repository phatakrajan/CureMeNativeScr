Granny Says - My hair is still jet black. If you also want hair like me then follow my tips described below.

  • Take a little Henna, an egg, juice of half a lemon, one table spoon of instant coffee powder. Mix it together and apply on the hair. Wash it after 45 minutes.
  • Boil one cup of dried gooseberry with 4 cups of water. Add a pinch of sugar in it. Keep boiling till the quantity of the liquid reduces to one cup. Mix 2 cups of Henna, an egg, juice of a lemon and the gooseberry solution and apply on hair. Wash the hair after two hours.
