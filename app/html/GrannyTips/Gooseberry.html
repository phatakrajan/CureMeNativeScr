Indian Gooseberry known as 'Amla' is a wonder medicine in Ayurveda. Its benefit is not just rich source of vitamin C but also its specific composition , because of which it controls all the 3 elements (Doshas) 'Kapha', 'Pitta' and 'Vaat' in body.

It acts as 'Rasayana'. The medicines which regularize metabolic activities, improve immunity and strengthen the body are categorized as 'Rasayana'.

Amla is used in many diseases such as Acidity, Colitis, Urinary tract infections, cough, diabetes, wounds. It improves liver function, thus quality of blood and 'Shukra Dhatu' (the reproductive functions.) Other benefits include sharper mind, memory, fairer complexion, youthful and healthy long life.

  • As fresh fruits/fruit slices/juice (1-2 tea spoons/day)
  • Chyavanprash.
  • Moravala (Amla jam).
  • As dry powder (choorna).
  • In Sherbats and pickles (Eat pickle in moderate quantity).

The best time to have Amla is in early morning, empty stomach. Nothing should be had half an hour after that. For best results have it for long duration (every day)
