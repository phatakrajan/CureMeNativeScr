Description -
Acne treatment is the most sought after treatment teenagers. Acne is a general and chronic skin ailment. The lesions are generally found on the skin of the face, neck, chest and shoulders.

Symptoms -
Acne symptoms include the presence of different types of pimples and cysts on the affected area of the skin.

Causes -

  • Teenagers and young adolescents show an increased manifestation of acne as the hormonal changes during puberty add to the sebum build-up, leading to more inflamed pores.
  • In girls and women, the timeline during the menstrual cycle shows an increase in acne on the skin.
  • Hormonal changes at the onset of puberty or during pregnancy can also cause the pores on the skin to get clogged up.
  • A sudden start or stop in the use of birth control medication can also result in acne showing up on the skin.
  • Lifestyle changes, especially being subject to a lot of stress, can also result in acne developing on the skin.
  • Heredity and the use of greasy cosmetics also results in the appearance of acne.

How to avoid -

  • Expose your body to sun and air, but do not sunburn.
  • Fresh air and daily exercise are very important. Get sufficient sleep. Keep your bowels regular.
  • Keep your bedsheets clean as dead skin, oil, cosmetics, hair, dirt on the sheets will clog your pores further.
  • Wash your face twice or thrice a day. Avoid using soaps that dry your skin.
  • Avoid oil-based cosmetics, as they tend to clog the pores on your skin.
  • Wash your hair every third or fourth day.

Self Help -

  • Do not pinch the pimple as it can lead to inflammation and can leave scars on your face. However, if you are desperate, then use a hot, clean cloth or tissue to soften the pimple. This will allow you to break the pimple open with gentle pressure.
  • Clean your skin thoroughly and apply egg white with cotton swab. Let it stay for several hours or overnight. Egg white acts as an astringent.

  • Place turmeric powder or basil leaves in a bowl of water, which has just been boiled. Cover your hair with a towel. Create a mini-steam bath for your face. Make sure to wash your face immediately afterwards, as dirt and oil can be removed more easily from open pores.
  • Apply lemon juice and rose water in equal amount with a cotton ball and leave for half an hour and wash. Repeat this remedy for 20-25 days.

  • Take small amount of ground almonds and honey and make a fine paste. Gently massage this paste on your face with a hot washcloth and then rinse with cold water.
  • Mix 1/2 cup mashed apple, 1/2 cup cooked oatmeal, 1 slightly beaten egg white and 1 tablespoon lemon juice into a fine paste and apply to your face for at least 15 minutes to 20 minutes and rinse with cold water.
