How to avoid -

  • Improve your diet to include fruits and vegetables, especially in raw form.
  • Drink 8 to 10 glasses of water daily.
  • Avoid sweets, caffeine, alcohol, tobacco, and junk foods.
  • Avoid rancid oils. Refrigerate all after opening.
  • Have nuts and seeds stored in freezer or refrigerator. Store grains in cool places.
  • Avoid all fried foods as the hot cooking oils contain substances that damage the skin.
  • Take beta-carotene rich foods such as carrots, spinach, sweet-potatoes, pumpkin, mango, cauliflower etc.
  • Take potassium-rich foods like banana, potato, coconut-water, cur, soybeans, orange, pumpkin, sweet potato, almonds, spinach, ground nuts etc.
  • Wheat flour, chicken liver, oysters, sunflower seeds etc. contain reasonable amounts of selenium which will also help in this condition.
  • Consume Vitamin E rich foods like almonds, cashew nuts, groundnuts, soya beans, sunflower oil, sesame oil, groundnut oil etc. Vitamin E is an antioxidant that slows aging and aids in tissue repair.
